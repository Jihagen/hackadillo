About Me

I'm a Data Science Research Assistant and Student. I have an affection for abstractions, theory and synaesthetic pattern-matching across seemingly unrelated disciplines.

Currently, I'm honing my skills at Siemens Healthineers building for ML and NLP use-cases.

I have lots of fun prototyping at Hackathons and for private projects. My favorite procrastination is getting into knowledge systems like building a second brain or refining my Spotify playlists. I practically live with my VR headset on while working.

In my downtime, you can find me traveling, indulging in food, or engaging in a variety of sports like hiking, climbing, and tennis. I also love a good cup of coffee, a game of chess, and spending time with my lovely partner and dog.
